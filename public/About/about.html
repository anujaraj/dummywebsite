About Our School

Girls' Government Inter College,Talbehat(Lalitpur) started as a primary school in the year 1905. It was later converted into a senior secondary school in the year 1977. The building was inaugurated on 17th May, 1993 by Mr Nawal Singh, the then Deputy Director of Jhansi

The first Principal of the senior secondary institute was Mrs Dayawati Das(1977-79)

We believe in strong character building of those new buds which shall blossum in the world in times to come
